Welcome to Course Connect!

What is Course Connect?

Course Connect is a service that makes it easier for you to find courses.

We were not given access to CTEC data so we are getting our data from you! Submit ratings and help improve our suggestions!

How do I use Course Connect?

To get the best experience out of Course Connect, use the "What courses do I take?" feature to get courses we think you'll enjoy based on what you've taken in the past.

Course Connect also features a class rating system that allows us to better understand what you like and don't like about classes.
